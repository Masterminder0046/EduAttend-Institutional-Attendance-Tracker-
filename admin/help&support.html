Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How to reset my password?

Go to Settings → Change Password. If forgotten, contact admin.

Q. How to mark attendance?

Faculty can mark attendance in the Faculty Dashboard under “Mark Attendance”.

Q. Can I download attendance reports?

Yes, go to Reports → Click “Export CSV”.
